  • Hypothesized that contact lenses may be associated with discomfort related to dry eyes due to an increase in the evaporation of tears.
  • Tested in vitro; after finding that rates of evaporation through contact lenses increased by 40%, worked to reverse the effect.
  • Analyzed catalogues of features to create timelines that would demonstrate evolutions from personal communication to content distribution.
  • Squalene, a natural moisturizer and deficient in those with dry eye, decreases rate of evaporation by over 60% leading to a finding that could potentially revolutionize the contact industry and provide relief to those adversely affected by contact lens wear.
